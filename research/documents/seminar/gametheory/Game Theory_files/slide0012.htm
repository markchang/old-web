Bottled Water Game
¥Players: Evian, Perrier
¥Each company has a fixed cost of $5000 per period, regardless of sales
¥They are competing for the same market, and each must chose a high price ($2/bottle), and a low price ($1/bottle)
¥At $2, 5000 bottles can be sold for $10,000
¥At $1, 10000 bottles can be sold for $10,000
¥If both companies charge the same price, they split the sales evenly between them
¥If one company charges a higher price, the company with the lower price sells the whole amount and the higher price sells nothing
¥Payoffs are profits Ð revenue minus the $5000 fixed cost
