Conclusions
• Pros
• Evolved circuits can be much more compact and power-
efficient than human-created circuits
• Intrinsic evolution allows us to explore and exploit physical
dynamics of hardware without the need to understand the
exact dynamic behavior
• Evolving circuits can be exposed to robustness parameters
and can evolve tolerances and adaptability
• Evolution can take place without human intervention
• Evolved circuits can teach designers new tricks
• Cons
• Fitness function creation can be a non-trivial task
• Intrinsic fitness evaluation can be time consuming
• Very little understanding of fundamentals
