Notes:

    A/D: Fourteen A/D conmverters to generate the digital numbers (DN) from the focal planes. Ground tests measure deviations from linearity for each A/D converter. Then, we correct for quantization effects with a table lookup.
    Zero: Use SV port
    Temperature: Temperature coefficient (K) determined for each detector on ground. MODIS will warm up over time because the thermal blanket loose efficiency.
    Scan angle: Reflectance depends on the angle at which the mirror is viewed. Use the normal to the scan mirror surface. SRCA and SD data are mapped into the normal.
