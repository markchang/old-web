About

I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Washington, my M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Northwestern University, and my B.S. from Johns Hopkins University.

My current and past research areas include mobile, social, and ubiquitous computing; computer-aided design tools; reconfigurable devices; FPGA arithmetic and architecture. More details available on the research page.
